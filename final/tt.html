TRI-TECH | ADVANTAGES & DISADVANTAGES

Tri-Tech as a Skills Center succeeds immensely at providing insight and experience for a profession. The education feels very thorough and can be well utilized for the future. The environment feels very preparing, and peers are very cooperative with the process.

The only issues appear to be its location and integration into traditional high school. Many have difficulty commuting from around the area due to distance or other stipulations, and attendance otherwise is not always convenient for the schedule of the home high school.
